Our Story

At Time Recorders, we are passionate about mechanical watches and are dedicated to sharing our knowledge with our customers. Our team of experts is committed to providing the best information and resources on mechanical watches, as well as offering a selection of budget-friendly timepieces. Whether you are a seasoned collector or new to the world of watches, we are here to help you understand what makes a mechanical watch tick and how to care for it. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to own a well-made watch, regardless of their budget.
